Clean Bulk: calorie surplus from mostly whole, nutrient-dense foods. Read more

Clean Bulk: Focus on lean proteins (eggs, paneer, chicken, legumes), complex carbs (rice, potatoes, oats), healthy fats (nuts, olive oil). Aim for a modest surplus (+250–350 kcal), progressive overload in training, high protein (~1.6–2.2 g/kg), and good sleep. Expect slower but leaner gains.

Fast Bulk: Larger surplus (+400–700 kcal) and less strict food selection to gain size quicker. Expect faster scale increases with a bit more fat. Keep protein high, train hard, and monitor waist measurements so you can dial back if fat gain is too quick.

Smart Weight Gain Tips

  • Track your weekly average weight; aim for +0.25–0.5 kg per week.
  • Prioritize progressive overload: add reps, sets, or weight weekly.
  • Eat protein at every meal and don’t skip post-workout nutrition.
