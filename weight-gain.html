Weight Gain — Build Lean Mass

Eat a small calorie surplus, prioritize protein, and lift progressively for slow, steady gains.

1. +200–300 kcal/day

Add extra calories from nuts, dairy, avocado, and whole grains.

2. Protein focus

Target ~1.6–2.2 g/kg bodyweight/day from paneer, tofu, dal, dairy, soy.

3. Lift 3–4× week

Progressive overload on squats, presses, rows, hinges. Track sets/reps.

4. Recovery

Sleep 7–9h, manage stress, and don’t skip rest days.

Simple 2-Day Split (repeat)

  • Upper: Push-ups, Pike push-ups, Rows, Curls, Triceps dips
  • Lower: Squats, Lunges, Glute bridges, Calf raises, Planks
Tip: Use the tracker to ensure you actually hit your surplus daily.
