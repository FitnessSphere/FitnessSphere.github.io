Weight Loss — Start Smart

Aim for steady fat loss with a small calorie deficit, regular movement, and strength training to maintain muscle.

1. Set your target

Pick a realistic pace: ~0.25–0.5 kg per week. Extreme diets backfire.

2. Track intake

Use the Calories page. Focus on protein, veggies, whole grains, and water.

3. Move daily

Combine steps/cardio with 2–3 strength sessions weekly to protect muscle.

4. Sleep & stress

7–9h sleep, manage stress — both affect hunger and recovery.

Sample 3-Day Rotation

  • Day A (Strength): Squats, Push-ups, Rows, Planks — 3×8–12
  • Day B (Cardio): Brisk walk or cycle 30–40 min + mobility
  • Day C (HIIT): 6 rounds: 30s burpees / 60s rest + core

Repeat A→B→C with at least one easy day per week.

Tip: Consistency beats perfection. Save your days in the tracker and watch the 7-day chart.
